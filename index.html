How this works?

Build A 3d Website

Build your 3d website using three.js, HTML and CSS. (use the guide :3)

Publish Your Site

Publish the website using Vercel(recomended :D), and show it to the world

Go Watch Your Movie!

Submit the website you built, and we will send you a grant to buy tickets!

Book Your Tickets!

A 10$ Grant

Minimum of 2 hours and 1 geometric shapes/models required on a project

A 15$ Grant

Minimum of 3 hours and 2 geometric shapes/models required on a project

A 20$ Grant

Minimum of 4 hours and 4 geometric shapes/models required on a project

Requirements

  • You are a high school student (under 18)
  • Your website must include content and have meaning
  • No AI is allowed to be used to make the website
  • Your website should use CSS styling
  • Your website should contain at-least 1 shape
  • Your website should be ONLY made by Three.js
