How will the workshop work?

Run A Workshop With Your Club

Submit a request to host a workshop with your club and wait for it to get activated by the reviewers

Members Build & Host Their Site

Use the guide to help your members build and host their 3d website onto the internet

Get Your Movie Grant!

Submit the websites that your members made here! and we will send your members a grant to buy tickets!

Note: After you are done with your workshop and have submitted the websites, you can then either choose to close the workshop or keep it open. Keep in mind that it is adviced to keep the workshop open only if you are planning to submit more websites to it. You can easily close your workshop by dm'ing me(@zeeno.) or @jps on the slack or by shooting an email @ zaidayan456@gmail.com!

Book Your Tickets!

Each of your club members will get a 15$ grant to go watch the movies (or, get reimbursed for the movies if you want to buy it with your own card), PROVIDED that your club members submit a valid project that meets the requirements, through the form mentioned above ^

Requirements

  • You are a high school student (under 18)
  • Your website should be made only using vanilla CSS and JS
  • Your website must include content and have meaning
  • No AI is allowed to be used to make the website
  • Your website should use CSS styling
  • Your website should contain at-least 2+ shapes
  • Your website should contain a minimum of 1 texture
  • Your website should be ONLY made by Three.js

Frequently Asked

  • Where do I ask for help? : For questions, ask in #polygon in slack. For questions related to Polygon, DM @zeeno. on the Slack. For questions about club meetings, DM @jps on the Slack

  • How do I make a 3d website? : Making a 3d website is actually very simple, if you don't know how to make one then following the guide would be helpful. You can find it in the Resources mentioned above.

  • What can I buy for this grant? : You are supposed to buy movie tickets to your favourite movie (ps: if you have some money still left on your grant after buying the tickets, then you can spend the rest on popcorn!)

  • Can I use anything besides three.js? : You are supposed to ONLY use three.js for your website, any other library for the project won't be accepted and you will not a get a grant unfortunately :C
